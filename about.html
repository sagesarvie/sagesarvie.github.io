sage sarvie is a multidisciplinary designer from ottawa, canada now based in the usa. she studied architectural conservation & sustainability engineering at carleton university and has industry experience in ontario (canada), irvine, new york, and seattle.

she was part of the season 0 digital artistry residence at web3 company wildxyz and her surrealist dreamscapes have been featured on hypebae, visual atelier 8, and in two separate exhibitions at the seattle nft museum.
